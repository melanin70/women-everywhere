Welcome to single mothers community

To those of you who are expecting or are already a single mother, I want to say: "You're not alone"

There are many other women who have chosen to become mothers without a partner or life changes that came up and led to it. We are here to support each other, share experiences and tips, or just a listening shoulder.


-Being a single mother can be challening sometimes but also a blessing. It is also an opportunity to create a strong -__independent relationship with your child.


Here are some things that might be good to think about if you are a single mother:
¨seek support¨ It could be from ¨family, friends, a support group or a professional therapist.¨

Take care of yourself. It is important that you feel well both physically and mentally, for both you and the child. Don't be afraid to ask for help. There are many resources available for single mothers. Here are some tips that can make life as a single mother easier from us to you: Create routines. Routines provide security and structure. Learn to delegate. It's okay to ask for help from others. Take breaks. It's important to take time for yourself, even if it's just for a little while. Be proud of yourself Being a single mother is a big task. You are strong, beautiful and a fantastic role model for your children, we are here for you. Welcome to the community!

Activitiec for you and your little one!

Hiking adventure

Hiking with kids can feel boring some times alone but it can also be a wonderful way to spend time togheter outdoors, exploring nature and all its beauty. So why not do it with us and our mom group 2 a week. There will be snacks and drinks available for the whole group. So plz leet us know about any allergies, so we may arange someting that will work.

Baking

Baking allows kids to express their creativity through decorating cakes,cupcakes,cookies and other pastries. They can experiment with different colors,shapes and designs and it gives them hands-on activity that engages all of the senses. It is also an opportunity for bonding and social interaction.

Mommy Talk

Where we talk, listen and give support/tips about our day to day overwhelmes.

Mommy and me spa

A mommy and kid spa experience can be a wonderful way for mothers and children to bond, relax and pamper yourselves together. It is also a screen free zone to encourage mindfulness and presence. And of course we offer a 1 to 2 hour free time where the kids play together with supervision off our employees while mommy can either take a nap or continue relaxing in the spa.
